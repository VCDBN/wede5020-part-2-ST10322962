OUR STRATEGY

our "Clean Air for All" Strategy outlines how we are advancing the fight against global air pollution.

From public health to climate change, child development to sustainable economic growth, improving our air quality can help address some of society's most pressing problems. We are enacting bold change at all levels, from communities to governments, to ensure everyone has access to clean air.
We support and collaborate with organizations all across the world that advance data, increase public demand for clean air, and spark action. By uniting funders, academics, policymakers, businesses, and campaigners to support the cause of improved air quality, we encourage and assist decision-makers in taking action on clean air. We also assist areas where air pollution has an outsized impact on residents.
